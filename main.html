The Shawshank Redemption

Actors Quotes
The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 drama film based on the novella "Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption" by Stephen King. It tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for a crime he didn't commit. The film follows his journey as he befriends fellow inmates and works to escape from prison.

Actors

The film stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding. Both actors deliver outstanding performances that contribute to the film's enduring legacy.
